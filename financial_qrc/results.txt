=== QRC Financial Direction Classifier — SPY Results ===
Date: April 2026
Data: SPY daily returns (499 trading days, ~2 years)
Task: Predict next-day market direction (Up=1, Down=0)

--- Qubit Sweep Results ---
Qubits    QRC Acc    Naive Baseline    Beat Baseline?
2         53.7%      52.6%             YES (+1.1%)
3         53.7%      52.6%             YES (+1.1%)
4         51.6%      52.6%             NO  (-1.0%)
5         50.5%      52.6%             NO  (-2.1%)

--- Classical Baseline (SVM on raw signals, window=5) ---
Accuracy: 48.4%

--- Key Findings ---
1. Small QRC (2-3 qubits) slightly beats naive baseline.
2. Larger QRC (4-5 qubits) hurts performance — overfits noise.
3. QRC and classical SVM perform comparably on this task.
4. Consistent with Efficient Market Hypothesis — SPY direction
   has weak short-term autocorrelation signal.
5. Reservoir size is a critical hyperparameter for financial QRC.
